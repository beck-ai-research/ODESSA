# ODESSA Research Directory Structure

This structure is designed for systematic OSINT and historical analysis of the ODESSA organization and its networks.

- `research/`
    - `entities/` (Profiles on individuals, organizations, and Nazi escape networks)
        - `DACH/` (Germany, Austria, Switzerland focus - e.g., Stille Hilfe)
        - `LATAM/` (South American connections - e.g., Argentina, Chile)
        - `ITALY/` (Mafia networks and collaboration nodes)
        - `GLOBAL/` (International funding and logistics)
    - `narratives/` (Analysis of ODESSA origins, operations, and legacy)
        - `odessa-origin/`
        - `ratline-logistics/`
        - `funding-networks/`
        - `mafia-collaboration/`
        - `modern-successors/`
    - `technical_osint/` (Data from Chrome DevTools MCP for tracking modern leads)
        - `network_logs/`
        - `metadata_scans/`
        - `infrastructure_mapping/`
    - `legal/` (Declassified documents, trial transcripts, registry entries)
- `templates/` (Standardized markdown templates)
- `NARRATIVE_CHECK.md` (Central evidence tracker)
- `AGENTS.md` (Operational protocols)
